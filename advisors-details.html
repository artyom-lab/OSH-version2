About Me
In. Abdulwahab A. Al Maimani is the Founder & GM of Abdulwahab's Office. Additionally, he is the Chairman of Board of Al Muhandis Group, a founder of nine firms, and shareholder in more than twelve enterprises. Remarkably, he was recognised as one of the World’s Leader Businessperson by the World Confederation of Businesses. He started a firm, and with proper partnerships, the group of companies now consists of nine companies with a market value of more than $7.1Millions in less than 3 years. He created the First "Digital International Youth Forum" with partnership of UNGC, and creator of Jawhar Digital Library and Repository of Knowledge & Creatives. Previously, we won several achievements in projects related to the tourism sector, Lean & Six Sigma, Process Improvement, Marketing, Sales, Engineering Design, and Creative Facility Planning and Management. He is member of Oman American Business Association, World Confederation of Businesses, WCFAC, IEOM, UNGC, Industrial Advisory Board Member at iLC, and Alumni Advisory Board Member at College of Engineering SQU.
